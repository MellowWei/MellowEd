Core Theory

Learning as Rhythm Return

I frame learning not as simple knowledge transfer, but as a rhythm-based process where perception, time, body, language, and environment co-produce the learner’s state.

1

Institutional Rhythm / 制度节律

Schools organize learning through deadlines, grading, standardized expression, class schedules, assessment formats, and legitimate language. These structures do not only measure learning; they shape what kinds of learning can appear.

2

Lived Learning Rhythm / 生命学习节律

Every learner has a real rhythm made of attention, emotion, body state, memory, sensory density, music, recovery, and motivation. This rhythm is not noise. It is the living condition of learning.

3

Rhythm Mismatch / 节律错位

Many learning difficulties are not failures of intelligence or discipline. They are mismatches between institutional rhythm and lived learning rhythm, especially for ADHD learners, high-sensitivity learners, and emotionally overloaded learners.

4

Rhythm Return / 节律返回

Education should help learners perceive, name, regulate, and design their own learning rhythms. The goal is not compression. The goal is return.

Abstract / 摘要

Human Return Learning is my education contribution portal. It frames learning not as institutional compression, but as the learner’s return to their own rhythm. My work connects learning sciences, qualia-rhythm theory, music-based state regulation, AI as reflective learning mirror, participatory education, and human-machine music interfaces. I argue that many learning difficulties are not simply failures of intelligence, discipline, or motivation, but rhythm mismatches between institutional time and lived learning rhythm. Through projects such as BCI-HRP, Qualia Rhythm Matrix, AiQ愛<7, Astro Time Candle, and genre-driven music learning systems, I build a new educational framework where learners can perceive, name, regulate, and redesign their own learning states.

Human Return Learning 是我的教育贡献母站。它把学习定义为学习者返回自身节律的过程，而不是被制度压缩的过程。我的工作连接学习科学、感质-节律理论、音乐状态调节、AI 学习镜面、参与式教育和人机音乐接口。我认为许多学习困难并不只是智力、自律或动机问题，而是制度时间与真实生命学习节律之间的错位。通过 BCI-HRP、Qualia Rhythm Matrix、AiQ愛<7、Astro Time Candle 和音乐类型驱动的学习系统，我建立一种新的教育框架：让学习者重新感知、命名、调节并设计自己的学习状态。
Four-Layer Analysis

命名行为 / 加压行为 / 滑义行为 / 相变行为

This is the internal logic of the website: it does not merely display my education work. It changes the category of what my education work is.

Layer 01

命名行为 / Naming

I do not call this an education portfolio. I name it Human Return Learning. The name shifts education from knowledge delivery to rhythm return.

Layer 02

加压行为 / Intensification

My projects intensify one another: theory becomes interface, interface becomes sound, sound becomes learning state, learning state becomes system.

Layer 03

滑义行为 / Meaning Shift

Learning slides from study to perception, from perception to rhythm, from rhythm to body, from body to AI, from AI to human return.

Layer 04

相变行为 / Phase Transition

When placed together, my education work is no longer a set of class assignments. It becomes a new educational system ready for professors, labs, and collaborators.
