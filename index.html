Abstract / 摘要

Human Return Learning as research, prototype, and educational system.

Human Return Learning is my education research and prototype portal. It develops the Qualia-Rhythm System, a framework that redefines learning through perception, music, individual rhythm, collective rhythm, institutional rhythm, and learner agency. I argue that many learning difficulties are not simply failures of intelligence, discipline, or motivation, but rhythm mismatches between institutional time and lived learning rhythm.

Human Return Learning 是我的教育研究与原型入口。它发展出 Qualia-Rhythm System，一个通过感知、音乐、个体节律、集体节律、制度节律与学习者主体性重新定义学习的框架。我认为，许多学习困难并不只是智力、自律或动机问题，而是制度时间与真实生命学习节律之间的错配。

Through participatory education, RPP/RCPP theory, YPAR, justice-centered systems change, and my own design practice, I extend educational theory in four directions: from voice to qualia legitimacy, from power-sharing to rhythm-sharing, from equity-centered data to rhythm-sensitive data, and from music as background to music as learning infrastructure. AiQ愛<10 translates this framework into an AI-based educational prototype that helps learners regulate attention, process emotion, and regain mastery over their own learning rhythm.

通过参与式教育、RPP/RCPP 理论、YPAR、justice-centered systems change 与我的设计实践，我将教育理论推进到四个方向：从 voice 到 qualia legitimacy，从 power-sharing 到 rhythm-sharing，从 equity-centered data 到 rhythm-sensitive data，从背景音乐到学习基础设施。AiQ愛<10 则把这一框架转化为一个 AI 教育原型，帮助学习者调节注意力、承接情绪，并重新掌握自身学习节律。

Theory / 核心理论

Qualia-Rhythm System

A framework for understanding learning as a process of perception, rhythm, music, legitimacy, and return.

一个把学习理解为感知、节律、音乐、合法性与返回过程的教育框架。

01 · Qualia

Perception as the beginning of learning

Learning begins from perception: affective intensity, embodied experience, sensory texture, attention, emotion, and subjective feeling.

学习从感知开始：情绪强度、具身体验、感官质地、注意力、情绪与主观感受，都是学习发生的真实条件。

02 · Rhythm

Rhythm as educational infrastructure

Rhythm is not only pace. It includes musical rhythm, individual rhythm, collective rhythm, institutional rhythm, and the temporal structure of knowledge production.

节律不只是快慢。它包括音乐节律、个体节律、集体节律、制度节律，以及知识生成本身的时间结构。

03 · Institutional Rhythm

How schools organize visibility

Schools organize learning through schedules, deadlines, standardized pacing, assessment formats, and legitimate language.

学校通过课程表、deadline、统一 pacing、评价格式与合法语言来组织学习。

04 · Lived Rhythm

The real rhythm of learners

Every learner has a real rhythm shaped by attention, emotion, memory, body state, music, recovery, sensory density, and motivation.

每个学习者都有真实的学习节律，由注意力、情绪、记忆、身体状态、音乐、恢复、感官密度与动机构成。

05 · Rhythm Mismatch

Difficulty is not always deficiency

Many learning struggles are not failures of intelligence or discipline. They are mismatches between institutional rhythm and lived learning rhythm.

许多学习困难并不是智力或自律失败，而是制度节律与生命学习节律之间的错位。

06 · Rhythm Return

Education as return

Education should help learners perceive, name, regulate, and redesign their own learning rhythms.

教育应帮助学习者感知、命名、调节并重新设计自己的学习节律。

Mini-Inquiry / Participatory Education Methods

Rewriting educational inquiry through perception, music, and learning rhythm.

This mini-inquiry transforms course readings, reflective inquiry, and design practice into a research pathway for the Qualia-Rhythm System.

Project Title

Qualia-Rhythm System: Rewriting Educational Inquiry Through Perception, Music, and Learning Rhythm

感质—节律系统：通过感知、音乐与学习节律重写教育探究。

Key Finding

Many students’ struggles are misread as inability, disengagement, or lack of motivation, when they may actually reflect a mismatch between lived learning rhythm and institutional rhythm.

许多学生的困难常被误读为能力不足、缺乏动力或不够投入，但更深层上，它们可能是生命学习节律与制度节律之间的错配。

Research Questions
  • 01. How do educational institutions flatten learners’ perception and rhythm through their time structures and legitimacy structures?

    教育制度如何通过它的时间结构和合法表达结构，压平学习者原本的感知与节律？

  • 02. To what extent are students’ “difficulties” actually mismatches between lived learning rhythm and institutional rhythm, rather than ability problems?

    很多学生表现出来的“困难”，是否并不是能力问题，而是 lived learning rhythm 与 institutional rhythm 之间的错配？

  • 03. How can music, perception, and rhythm become educational entry points that help learners regain mastery over their own learning state and pathway?

    音乐、感知与节律，能否成为帮助学习者重新掌握自身学习状态和路径的教育入口？

Educational Upgrades / 我的教育升级

From participation to rhythm-sensitive education.

I do not simply repeat participatory inquiry, YPAR, RPP/RCPP, or justice-centered systems change. I upgrade them through perception, music, rhythm, and learner state.

UPGRADE 01

From Voice to Qualia Legitimacy

Student voice matters, but educational justice cannot stop at voice. Silence, hesitation, overload, numbness, sensory intensity, musical attachment, and rhythm mismatch are also meaningful learning signals.

从声音到感质合法性。学生声音重要，但教育正义不能停在 voice。沉默、犹豫、过载、麻木、感官强度、音乐依附与节律错位，也都是有意义的学习信号。

UPGRADE 02

From Power-Sharing to Rhythm-Sharing

Participation remains incomplete if institutions still monopolize pace, timing, sequence, and what counts as timely contribution.

从权力共享到节律共享。如果制度仍然垄断 pace、timing、sequence 和什么算“及时参与”，那么参与仍然是不完整的。

UPGRADE 03

From Equity Data to Rhythm-Sensitive Data

If data systems only track grades, punctuality, compliance, and outputs, they will continue to miss the rhythms and states through which learning actually happens.

从公平中心数据到节律敏感、感质合法的数据。如果数据系统只记录成绩、准时、服从和结果，它会继续错过学习真正发生时的节律、状态与感知过程。

UPGRADE 04

From Background Music to Learning Infrastructure

Music is not decoration. It is a medium for attention regulation, emotional processing, state transition, and rhythm mastery.

从背景音乐到学习基础设施。音乐不是装饰，而是调节注意力、承接情绪、转换状态、掌握节律的学习基础设施。

Prototype / AI Rhythm Mirror

AiQ愛<10

Qualia-Rhythm Learning Interface · Human Return AI · Music-Supported State Regulation

Prototype / 原型系统

AiQ愛<10 translates the theory into interface design.

AiQ愛<10 is an AI-based educational prototype that translates the Qualia-Rhythm System into interface design. It treats scroll velocity, click density, dwell time, pause, input rhythm, and interaction intensity not as noise, but as meaningful signals of learner state.

AiQ愛<10 是一个把感质—节律系统转化为界面设计的 AI 教育原型。它把滚动速度、点击密度、停留时长、停顿、输入节律与互动强度视为学习者状态信号，而不是噪音。

Through music, reflective language, and rhythm-sensitive feedback, AiQ helps learners regulate attention, process emotional intensity, enter focus, and gradually regain mastery over their own learning rhythm.

通过音乐、反思语言与节律敏感反馈，AiQ 帮助学习者调节注意力、承接情绪强度、进入专注，并逐步重新掌握自己的学习节律。

Music Learning Matrix / 音乐学习矩阵

Music as rhythm mastery.

In the Qualia-Rhythm System, music is not background sound. It is a learning infrastructure that helps learners regulate state, enter focus, process emotion, and regain mastery over their own rhythm. Each genre functions as a state-transition pathway.

在感质—节律系统中，音乐不是背景声音。音乐是一种学习基础设施，帮助学习者调节状态、进入专注、承接情绪，并重新掌握自己的节律。每一种音乐类型都是一条状态转换路径。

Genre / 音乐类型 Learning Function / 学习功能 Rhythm Mastery Pathway / 节律掌握路径
Breakbeats Uncertainty tolerance, anti-dissociation anchoring, flexible attention activation. Helps learners ride interruption instead of collapsing under it.
Hyperpop Peak traversal, intensity permission, high-density identity affirmation. Helps learners safely reach intensity, then release and reorganize.
Synthpop First breath, narrative return, emotional access through melody and predictability. Helps learners receive structure as relief rather than command.
Darkwave Shadow integration, emotional stabilization, mourning and depth without collapse. Helps learners descend, feel, and return with clarity.
Ambient Techno Spatial focus, environmental regulation, flow infrastructure. Helps learners stabilize attention through subtle repetition and atmosphere.
427Hz Baseline anchor, frequency alignment, return signal. Helps learners return to a stable inner rhythm after intensity or drift.
